What is diamond painting and how is it different from traditional painting?

Diamond painting is a relatively new art form that is similar to traditional cross-stitch or mosaic painting, but with one key difference: instead of using small pieces of colored fabric or tile, diamond painting uses tiny diamonds to create a vibrant, sparkly image.

While traditional cross-stitch and mosaic paintings can be quite beautiful, we can also be time-consuming and frustrating, as it can be difficult to get the small pieces of fabric or tile to stay in place. Diamond painting, on the other hand, is much easier and more forgiving, as the tiny diamonds adhere easily to the adhesive canvas and stay put until you’re ready to remove them.
